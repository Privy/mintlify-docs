The following checklist is your roadmap to getting started with Privy.

**Completed** | **Getting Started Task** | **Related Resource**  
---|---|---  
**Account Setup**  
| Create a Privy account | Sign up on [Privy.com](https://www.privy.com/)  
|  Connect your store/site | [Install Privy on your site](https://help.privy.com/article/73-install-privy-on-your-site)  
| Set up a sending domain for email | [Use a custom email domain](https://help.privy.com/article/33-use-a-custom-sending-domain)  
|  Update your business address | [Update your email footer address](https://help.privy.com/article/141-update-your-email-footer-address)  
|  Import existing subscribers/contacts | [Import and manage contacts](https://help.privy.com/article/50-import-and-manage-contacts)  
|  Connect and configure integrations | [Integration options](https://help.privy.com/article/177-overview-privy-integrations)  
**Build Core Convert Displays**  
|  Create a welcome discount display |  [Create a welcome discount display](https://help.privy.com/article/488-create-a-welcome-discount-display) [Convert displays examples](https://www.privy.com/customer-examples)  
|  Create a cart saver display |  [Create a cart saver display](https://help.privy.com/article/489-create-a-cart-saver-display)  
|  Create a cross-sell display |  [Create a cross-sell display](https://help.privy.com/article/508-create-a-cross-sell-display)  
|  Review other display ideas |  [Create a spin-to-win display](https://help.privy.com/article/518-create-a-spin-to-win-display) [Create a free shipping bar](https://help.privy.com/article/365-create-a-free-shipping-bar)  
|  Set up display targeting |  [ Use audience targeting conditions](https://help.privy.com/article/504-use-audience-targeting-conditions)  
|  Sync collected contacts to other tools using automation rules |  [Syncing contacts overview](https://help.privy.com/article/344-use-display-automation-rules)   
**Create Emails**  
| See if Privy Email is right for you |  [Overview of Privy Email](https://www.privy.com/email) [Getting Started with Privy Email](https://help.privy.com/article/46-get-started-privy-email)  
| Create an after-signup email |  [Set up an after-signup email](https://help.privy.com/article/545-set-up-an-after-signup-email)  
| Create abandoned cart emails |  [Send abandoned cart emails](https://help.privy.com/article/515-send-abandoned-cart-emails) [Abandoned cart examples](https://www.privy.com/customer-examples)  
| Create purchase follow-up emails |  [Send purchase follow-up emails](https://help.privy.com/article/131-send-purchase-follow-up-emails) [Purchase follow-up examples](https://www.privy.com/customer-examples)  
| Create a customer winback email |  [Send a customer winback email](https://help.privy.com/article/363-send-a-customer-winback-email) [Winback email examples](https://www.privy.com/blog/customer-winback-and-purchase-follow-up-emails-shopify)  
| Create a welcome email |  [Send welcome emails](https://help.privy.com/article/492-send-a-welcome-series) [Welcome email examples](https://www.privy.com/blog/how-to-welcome-email#welcome-email-examples)  
| Create a newsletter email |  [Send newsletter emails](https://help.privy.com/article/462-send-newsletter-emails) [Newsletter examples](https://www.privy.com/customer-examples)  
**Create Texts**  
| See if Privy Text is right for you |  [Overview of Privy Text](https://www.privy.com/text) [Getting Started with Privy Text](https://help.privy.com/article/101-get-started-with-privy-text)  
| Create an after-signup text | [Set up an after-signup text](https://help.privy.com/article/513-set-up-an-after-signup-text)  
| Create an abandoned cart text series |  [Send abandoned cart texts](https://help.privy.com/article/68-send-abandoned-cart-texts)  
| Create a one-time broadcast text | [Send a broadcast text](https://help.privy.com/article/455-send-a-broadcast-text)  
**Create Coupons**  
| Create coupons for your content |  [Create and manage coupon codes](https://help.privy.com/article/72-create-and-manage-coupon-codes) [Insert a coupon into your content](https://help.privy.com/article/28-insert-a-coupon-into-your-content)   
**Observe and Improve**  
| Get a snapshot of your performance | [Overview of the Privy homepage](https://help.privy.com/article/85-overview-of-the-privy-homepage)  
| Dig into your performance |  [Overview of Privy Reports](https://help.privy.com/article/25-overview-of-privy-reports) [Analyze display performance](https://help.privy.com/article/349-analyze-display-performance) [Analyze email performance](https://help.privy.com/article/226-analyze-email-performance)  
| Iterate and test your content |  [Create an A/B Test](https://help.privy.com/article/175-run-an-a-b-test-on-a-display)
