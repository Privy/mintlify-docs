# Get Started with Privy

Welcome to Privy! This guide will walk you through the essential steps to get your ecommerce marketing up and running with our platform.

## 1. Connect Your Ecommerce Platform

First, let's connect Privy to your online store:

1. Log in to your Privy account
2. Navigate to the 'Integrations' section
3. Select your ecommerce platform (e.g., Shopify, WooCommerce)
4. Follow the prompts to complete the connection

## 2. Import Your Contacts

If you have existing customer contacts, let's bring them into Privy:

1. Go to the 'Contacts' section in your dashboard
2. Click on 'Import Contacts'
3. Choose your file format and upload your contact list
4. Map your data fields and complete the import

## 3. Set Up Your Domain

Ensure proper tracking by setting up your domain records:

1. Go to 'Settings' > 'Domain Settings'
2. Copy the provided CNAME record
3. Add this record to your domain's DNS settings

## 4. Create Your First Sign-Up Form

Start growing your subscriber list:

1. Go to 'Forms' > 'Create New Form'
2. Choose a template or start from scratch
3. Customize the design and content
4. Set up display rules
5. Activate the form on your website

## 5. Launch a Campaign

Engage your audience with a marketing campaign:

1. Navigate to 'Campaigns' > 'Create New Campaign'
2. Select a campaign type
3. Design your email or SMS message
4. Choose your target audience
5. Schedule or send your campaign

## 6. Set Up an Automation

Create an automated sequence to nurture leads:

1. Go to 'Automations' > 'Create New Automation'
2. Choose a trigger event
3. Add email steps to your sequence
4. Set the timing for each step
5. Activate your automation
