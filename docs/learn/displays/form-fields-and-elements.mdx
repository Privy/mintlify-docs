When customizing your displays, you have the ability to add new elements (i.e., images or countdown timers) and new form fields (i.e., Name or Phone Number) to capture important information at sign up. This article will walk you through each element and form field supported by Privy, how to add them to your display, and your options for customizing them.

## Form Fields

Form fields collect information from your contacts as they sign up so that you can engage with your audience via the correct channels and personalize your content whenever possible! Below are the form fields supported by Privy today. *Click on a form field to jump ahead to its section in the article.*

1. Email

2. Phone

3. SMS Opt-In

4. Name

5. Checkbox

6. Radio Button

7. Mini Quiz
