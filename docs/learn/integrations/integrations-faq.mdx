**Privy doesn't integrate with my e-commerce site platform. Can I still use Privy?**

_Yes, you can manually install Privy on your website by _[_following these instructions_](https://help.privy.com/docs/learn/integrations/manual-install)_._

**I'm seeing an 'Unauthorized' error with one of my integrations. What do I do?**

_'Unauthorized' errors can occasionally happen, for a number of reasons. Learn more about the potential causes and how to resolve these errors with_ [_this guide_](https://help.privy.com/docs/learn/integrations/resolve-an-unauthorized-error)_._

**Does Privy work with single-page applications (SPA)?**

_Yes\! Learn more about our compatibility, advantages, and disadvantages in_ [_this resource_](https://help.privy.com/docs/learn/integrations/does-privy-work-with-spa#does-privy-work-with-spas)_._

**I'm using a third-party email service provider (ESP). Can I sync coupon codes from Privy sign ups to my ESP?**

_Yes,_ [_follow this guide_](https://help.privy.com/docs/learn/integrations/sync-coupons-to-esp) _for best practices on syncing coupon codes to your ESP as new contacts sign up._
