It's not possible to exclude visitors from seeing displays based on their
email addresses.

Instead, you can exclude visitors based on other attributes, such as whether
they are logged into your store or are a known contact within your Privy
account.

For help setting up your targeting check out our article on how to [Use
audience targeting
conditions.](https://privy.helpscoutdocs.com/article/70-use-audience-
targeting-conditions)
