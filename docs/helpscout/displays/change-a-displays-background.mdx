Whether you want to update one of the default templates or build a display
from scratch, adding or changing a display's background is quick and easy. To
customize your display:

  * Navigate to **Convert** > **All Displays** and choose or create a display. 
  * You will land in the **Create** step of the display builder.
  * In the _Elements_ sidebar, select the **Background Color** option from the right. 
  * Select the **Choose Image** button under the Background Image section to select or upload a new image. 

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/6606da67f4bb664508693b91/images/6684277fdd0f8c60bb3fdd66/file-
OMXDLwY5M0.gif)

  * Once you've made your changes, select **Save** at the top-right. 

All set! The background of your display is updated. If your display is already
published, the changes will go into effect immediately and appear to visitors
who qualify.
