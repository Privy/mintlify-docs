You can manage your personal account preferences and settings, such as your
display name, contact information, language, notifications, and password.
These settings will only affect your user profile and every account you access
with the provided email address.

To manage these settings, click your account name in the top right corner,
then **Account Settings**.

# Username and contact information

Users must update their names and contact information themselves. Other users,
even if they are the account owners, cannot edit this information on their
behalf.

To change your name and contact information:

  * Navigate to [My Information](https://dashboard.privy.com/settings/info). 
  * Click on the appropriate field.
  * Input the updated information.
  * Scroll down and click the **Save** button.

# Language preference

The language setting controls the language that is displayed in Privy's user
interface. Privy is currently available in English (US), Spanish (Castilian),
French (Metropolitan), and Portuguese (Brazilian).

To change the language that displays when you log into Privy:

  * Navigate to [Account Information](https://dashboard.privy.com/settings/info). 
  * Select the desired language option. 
  * Scroll down and click the **Save** button.

# Email notifications

Privy's email notifications provide insight into the performance of your
Convert displays and other activities in your account. The available options
include:

  * **Daily email updates** : This email provides insight into the views, signups, orders, and revenue generated by your active displays. It is sent each morning. 
  * **Weekly email updates** : This email provides insight into the views, signups, orders, and revenue generated by your active displays. It is sent each Monday morning. 
  * **Display summaries** : Provides insight into the performance of displays that have ended, such as those with a predetermined start and end time. 
  * **Autosync failures** : This feature provides an alert when a display fails to sync emails to a [Linked Account](https://dashboard.privy.com/settings/accounts) via the display's [automation rules](https://privy.helpscoutdocs.com/article/108-use-display-automation-rules). 

To manage your Privy notifications:

  * Navigate to [My Information](https://dashboard.privy.com/settings/info). 
  * Check the desired options. 
  * Scroll down and click the **Save** button. 

**Note:** Please add [notifications@privy.com](mailto:notifications@privy.com)
to your email address book to help ensure the delivery of these notifications.

# Password change

Learn how to change your password from within Privy or reset a forgotten
password.

To update your password from within Privy:

  * Navigate to [My Information](https://dashboard.privy.com/settings/info). 
  * Scroll down and click the **Change Password** button. 
  * Fill out the prompt and click **Save New Password**. 

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/6606da67f4bb664508693b91/images/66b4c7e448915f570c6a91ee/file-
OF5VtIOwIC.gif)

To update a forgotten password when you cannot log in:

  * Navigate to the [Privy Login](https://dashboard.privy.com/users/sign_in) page. 
  * Select the **Forgot your password** option. 
  * Enter the email address associated with your user account. 
  * Follow the steps sent to your email. The instructions come from [support@privy.com](mailto:support@privy.com). Please add this address to your email address book to ensure proper delivery. 

**Note:** The reset link included in the email expires after 6 hours.
