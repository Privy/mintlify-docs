Connect the Slack app to Privy to receive Slack notifications per display and
track new leads generated by your Privy Convert displays in real-time.

# Create a webhook URL

  * Navigate to the [Incoming WebHooks](https://privy.slack.com/apps/A0F7XDUAZ-incoming-webhooks?next_id=0) app in the Slack directory. 
  * Click the **Add to Slack** button. 

![](https://hf-files-
oregon.s3.amazonaws.com/hdpprivy_kb_attachments/2024/01-26/2fa785ab-6286-446d-8c40-53ca527b2a4e/Add_webhook.png)

  * On the next screen, choose the **Create a new channel** option underneath the " _Choose a channel_ " field. 

![](https://hf-files-
oregon.s3.amazonaws.com/hdpprivy_kb_attachments/2024/01-26/5092e7a0-a1f2-4378-aedf-
ee11a49f1c8e/Create_new_channel.png)

  * When prompted, give your channel a name, such as “privy-leads,” and then click the **Create** button. Once you’ve created your channel, you will be returned to the initial screen, where you should select the **Add Incoming Webhooks Integration** button.

![](https://hf-files-
oregon.s3.amazonaws.com/hdpprivy_kb_attachments/2024/01-26/d5bcec08-efdc-4f76-b0e3-aebd8850675f/Add_webhook_to_channel.png)

  * This action will bring you to another page that contains the **Webhook URL**. Copy the URL. 

![](https://hf-files-
oregon.s3.amazonaws.com/hdpprivy_kb_attachments/2024/01-26/66521328-c8d4-4b0b-a20e-00b90f0ab8db/Copy_webhook_URL.png)

# Add the webhook to Privy

  * In Privy, click the **Account** option from the navigation. Your business name appears there. 
  * Select the **Integrations** option from the dropdown menu. 
  * In the sidebar, select **Webhooks** under the _Integrations_ section. 
  * Select the **Slack** option and paste your webhook URL into the URL field. Also, set the _Method_ setting to "Post" and enable the _Signups_ and _Orders_ triggers. 

![](https://hf-files-
oregon.s3.amazonaws.com/hdpprivy_kb_attachments/2024/01-26/5c957d9d-0eb7-4f66-af6a-a0e1cc6bd9fc/Webhook_settings_in_Privy.png)

  * Click the **Save** option to confirm your configuration. After saving, you can test the connection. 

![](https://hf-files-
oregon.s3.amazonaws.com/hdpprivy_kb_attachments/2024/01-26/5908c5ef-4c9b-4162-b69c-a138cece1ea4/Test_the_webhook.png)

All set! You’re ready to see new leads generated by your Privy Convert
displays in real time.
