## Import your contacts

If you have existing customer contacts, import them into Privy via spreadsheet to start engaging with your audience right away.

## Register for SMS

If you're on our Growth Plan and planning on using Privy Text, your business needs to submit a registration form to be approved for a toll-free number. **You must register and be approved for your toll-free number before you can start collecting textable contacts and/or sending text messages.**

## Set up your domain records

To ensure the best deliverability possible + brand recognition with your audience, set up your custom sending domain in Privy.

Our team also recommends making sure that you have the proper DMARC, SPF, and DKIM authentication records in place so that you're meeting Google and Yahoo's sender requirements.

## Collect email and SMS contacts

Start growing your subscriber list with Privy Convert. Choose from a variety of templates with baked-in best practices, customize it to fit your brand, and activate on your site to start seeing results fast!

## Create your first campaign

Launch a marketing campaign to engage your audience:

1. Navigate to 'Campaigns' > 'Create New Campaign'
2. Select a campaign type (e.g., newsletter, promotion, product launch)
3. Design your email or SMS message
4. Choose your target audience
5. Schedule or send your campaign

## Set up an automated email sequence

Create an automated email sequence to nurture leads and drive conversions:

1. Go to 'Automations' > 'Create New Automation'
2. Choose a trigger event (e.g., new subscriber, purchase)
3. Add email steps to your sequence
4. Set the timing for each step
5. Activate your automation

## Integrate with other platforms

Enhance your marketing efforts by integrating Privy with other tools:

1. Visit the 'Integrations' section
2. Browse available integrations (e.g., Klaviyo, Mailchimp, Facebook)
3. Select the desired integration and follow the setup instructions
